How to Register a Cooperative

  1. Pay a non-refundable fee of N10,000 (Ten Thousand Naira) only into the bank account stated below with the Cooperative's name as the Depositor's Name or Narration/Description (if paying via Banking Mobile App):

    Bank: First Bank of Nigeria,
    Account Number: 0012345678,
    Account Name: MBTC Department of Cooperatives
  2. Send an email with "[COOPERATIVE NAME] REGISTRATION FEE PAYMENT" as the subject and the payment receipt (bank teller or mobile app receipt) as an attachment to email@edostate.gov.ng
  3. Upon receipt of the email and verification of payment, login details will then be sent to you to proceed with the registration here
  4. Please note that your login details is valid for ONLY ONE SESSION. What this means is that you can only use it to register one Cooperative Society. The moment you click "Finish" it becomes invalid and it's no longer reusable. Should you want to make changes thereafter, you'll need to contact the Ministry.
